🔍 Privacy overview

AppBooster: Download Increase is a powerful utility designed to optimize download speeds, improve network stability, and enhance your device's performance. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, why we need it, and how you remain in full control — because trust is the backbone of every boost.

Our promise: No selling of personal data, minimal permissions, full compliance with global privacy laws (GDPR/CCPA/COPPA). Your data stays yours.

📊 1. What data we collect

AppBooster is built to be lightweight and privacy-respecting. We only collect anonymous, non‑identifiable information to improve app performance and stability:

  • 📱 Device & technical info — model, OS version, language, country, advertising identifier (AAID / IDFA), IP address (anonymized). Used for analytics & ads.
  • 📡 Network & performance stats — connection type (Wi-Fi/5G/4G), signal strength, download speed metrics (aggregated). This helps us optimize the booster algorithm.
  • 📢 Ad interaction data — via Google AdMob. AdMob may use identifiers to serve contextual or non-personalized ads. No personal data is shared.
  • ⚙️ App usage preferences — theme choice, booster mode selection. Stored locally on your device.

We never access your personal files, contacts, location, or media gallery.

⚙️ 2. How we use your data

Your information helps us deliver the fastest, most reliable download boost experience:

  • Improve boost algorithms — analyze network conditions to optimize download acceleration.
  • Display relevant ads (optional banners and interstitials) to keep the app free for everyone.
  • Crash reporting & analytics — using Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs and enhance stability.
  • Save user preferences — like dark mode or boost sensitivity.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing.

🔌 3. Third‑party services (SDKs)

To provide core functionalities, we integrate with trusted partners:

  • Google AdMob – advertising. May collect device data and IP for ad relevance. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – anonymized crash reports and usage metrics. Helps us keep AppBooster ultra‑reliable.
  • Google Play Billing – if you choose to remove ads via in‑app purchase, Google Play processes the transaction. We never see payment details.

All third parties comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy frameworks.

🗑️ 4. Data retention & deletion

Your app preferences and local metrics remain on your device. Uninstalling AppBooster removes all local data. No server-side account exists. To request deletion of any analytics data linked to your advertising ID, email us at nadeemgamer180@gmail.com. We’ll respond within 30 days.

You can reset your advertising ID anytime: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID.

🎯 5. Your ad choices

We respect your advertising preferences:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • You may also purchase the “Remove Ads” feature (one‑time IAP) to enjoy an ad‑free experience.
  • For users under the age of consent, only non‑personalized contextual ads are shown.
